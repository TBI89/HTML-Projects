New to gardening? Discover the joy of growing your own ornamental plants

Once youve got a handle on the different types of plants - bulbs, annuals, perennials, shrubs and trees - you can start to put them together to gorgeous effect. But first, you need to decide on your planting style.

The top mistake that novice veg growers make is to grow too much. So start small - restrict yourself to a small salad patch, containers of veg on the patio or a small raised bed, then gradually expand your growing ambitions as time allows. Discover space-saving crops to grow.
